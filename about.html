About Us

What sort of restaraunt is this?

A unique gem located in the middle of nowhere, Faking Cane's is the first in a line of new restaraunts that sell classic dishes from fast food companies all over the US-- but in plastic! Our plasticized products can not only be used for pranks, but are delightful for display. We're working on biodegradeable food soon to help our envidonmental efforts! Come check us out!
